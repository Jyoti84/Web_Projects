Suggested books for you

Explore our top picks and find your next great read!

  • The Alchemist

    • Author: Paulo Coelho

    A philosophical story about following your dreams and listening to your heart.The Alchemist is an allegorical novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho. It tells the story of Santiago, a young Andalusian shepherd who dreams of discovering a hidden treasure near the Egyptian pyramids. Guided by omens and mysterious encounters—including a king, an Englishman, and an alchemist—Santiago embarks on a journey across deserts and foreign lands.

  • Atomic Habits

    • Author: James Clear

    Learn how small habits can create big changes in life and success.The book emphasizes focusing on systems rather than goals—instead of only aiming for big outcomes, you should design processes and routines that naturally lead to success. Clear also highlights the importance of identity-based habits, meaning lasting change happens when you see yourself as the kind of person who embodies those habits (e.g., “I am a healthy person,” not just “I want to lose weight”

  • Rich Dad Poor Dad

    • Author: Robert Kiyosaki

    A personal finance classic that teaches money management and financial freedom.The book teaches the importance of building assets, understanding money flow, and developing the mindset of financial independence. Instead of working only for a paycheck, Kiyosaki stresses learning how money works, creating passive income, and making money work for you.

  • A Book In Time

    • Author: Mark Stibbe

    A personal finance classic that teaches money management and financial freedom.It tells a captivating love story from a singular point of view: a rare first edition volume of poems, affectionately called The Burning Ones, longing across two centuries to be reunited with the elderly author who loved it as if it were her child
